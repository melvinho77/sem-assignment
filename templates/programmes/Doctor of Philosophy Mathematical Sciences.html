Programme Overview:

Department of Mathematical and Data Science at TAR UMT offers the opportunity to study for the degree of Doctor of Philosophy (Mathematical Sciences) in a thriving research environment. This programme served to those who are interested in research and require in-depth knowledge and experience in mathematics or statistics through individual and specialized research projects. The programme allow the students to acquire advanced knowledge in their fields of interest.

Intake:

Rolling Intake (throughout the year)

Duration:

Full time (Malaysian & International students) - 3 years

Part time (Malaysian students ONLY) - 4 years

Campus:

KL Main Campus

Compulsory

  • Research Methodology
  • Doctoral Research
  • Special Topic

Career Prospects

  • Business Consultant
  • Research Scientist (Maths)
  • Senior Data Scientist
  • Senior Academicians in Institutions of Higher Learning

Minimum Entry Requirements

  • A Master's Degree (MQF, Level 7) in Mathematics or Statistics or Mathematical Science related as accepted by the Senate; OR
  • Other qualifications equivalent to a Master's Degree (MQF, Level 7) in Mathematics or Statistics or Mathematical Science related that are accepted by the Senate of TAR UMT.

     

English Language Requirements

Malaysian Students

  • MUET Band 3.5 or IELTS Band 5.0 or CEFR High B1 or TOEFL iBT 40 or equivalent; OR
  • Exemption of English Language requirement if medium of instruction during Master studies was in English

International Students

  • IELTS Band 5.0 or CEFR High B1 or TOEFL iBT 40 or equivalent; OR
  • Exemption of English Language requirement if medium of instruction during Master studies was in English
